About Me

Building Ecosystems That Move Billions

I am a Strategic Partnerships & Alliances Leader with 9+ years turning complex commercial relationships into scalable, revenue-generating platforms across consumer technology, fintech, and digital commerce.

My career spans India's most recognized brands: OnePlus, Tira Beauty by Reliance Retail, FirstCry, and Tata Croma. I have built from 0 to 1 — Gift Card platforms, Co-branded Credit Card programs, BNPL infrastructure — and scaled each into a core business driver.

I bridge commercial strategy with operational execution. I don't just sign partnerships — I build governance frameworks, drive compliance, manage P&L, and ensure sustained partner value. Fluent in the language of banks, fintechs, marketplaces, product teams, and C-suites.
